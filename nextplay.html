Our Story

Zoomtech was launched to build community, create unprecedented access to career opportunity and promote Black culture in tech. We connect our community with career opportunities through networking events that blend recruiting, talks, art, live performances, and food/drinks. Culture and authenticity is at the core of everything we do. At our events everyone can be their authentic self, have genuine conversations, learn from C-Suite speakers and leave feeling inspired. We're addressing diversity recruiting challenges head on. Our employee referrals are 20x more likely to get hired. Our unique community first approach allows members to build relationships that ultimately lead to employee referrals.
